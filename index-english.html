Who are us

Bulnewsa sa, we are an Ecuadorian company that emerged in 2013, in response to the various needs of the local and foreign markets to find raw materials for the production of balanced feed, organic fertilizers and construction materials, of excellent quality at affordable prices, with logistical conditions suitable for the conservation of the product, being able to offer our clients personal and specialized attention.

MISSION

Provide raw materials for the production of balanced feed, organic fertilizers and quality construction materials to increase the competitiveness of our customers and satisfy them with professionalism through our products and services.

VISION

Being a first class company, globally recognized for the excellence of our products and the capacity of our people. Consolidate ourselves as a benchmark in the market, leading in all the commercial areas in which we operate.

OBJECTIVES

  • We seek to offer our people a work environment that they can feel proud of, to this end we constantly work on actions aimed at encouraging their human and professional development.
  • Growth and continuous improvement based on two pillars: innovation and creativity.
  • Commitment to the success of our clients, paying special attention to their specific requirements and needs of our markets.
  • Orientation of the individual objectives of our people within the framework of the global objectives of the organization for joint and continuous development with respect for society and the environment.
